Detailed Description:

The Sunflower Educational Complex located in Ward 11 has multiple damaged and partially fallen electrical distribution lines in and around the school campus. High-tension wires are hanging dangerously low over school pathways and playgrounds where hundreds of students and staff members move daily. The electrical lines have been damaged due to recent storms and heavy winds, with some lines sagging and rubbing against tree branches and building structures.

This creates an extremely dangerous situation where students could come into contact with live wires. The fallen and damaged lines are also causing intermittent power supply disruptions to the entire educational complex. Teachers report that the unstable power supply is affecting educational activities and digital classroom operations. The condition poses a potential catastrophic risk during monsoon season when falling branches could bring down the damaged lines completely onto the school premises.

Duration of Issue: Ongoing for the past 10 days

Impact on Citizens: Risk of electrocution for students and staff, disrupted educational services, unstable power supply, potential infrastructure collapse, and serious safety hazard in educational environment

Status: Emergency - Urgent Repair Required
