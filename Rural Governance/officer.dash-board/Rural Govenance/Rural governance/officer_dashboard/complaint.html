Detailed Description:

Residents of Shanti Nagar Residential Area, Ward 2 are experiencing severe electrical shocks and electric faults due to improper earthing and grounding systems. Multiple households report getting mild to severe electric shocks when touching metal fixtures, water taps, and electrical appliances. This dangerous condition poses a serious threat to human life and property safety.

The faulty earthing system is causing neutral-earth voltage issues making the entire water supply and metal infrastructure of homes electrically live. During rainy seasons, the situation worsens as moisture seepage increases the conductivity. Residents have reported incidents of family members getting electrocuted while bathing and touching water pipes, and pets being harmed by the live wiring. This is an urgent safety hazard that requires immediate intervention.

Duration of Issue: Ongoing for the past 5 weeks

Impact on Citizens: Life-threatening electrical shocks, risk of electrocution, property damage, inability to safely use water systems, and potential fatal accidents
